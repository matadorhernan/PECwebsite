ABOUT US

PEC Industrial is a Mexican company founded in 2002. We are located in Aguascalientes city, Ags, Mexico. We specialize in the developing of general automation, electromagnetic projects and industrial maintenance. We cover from basic concepts to the implementation of robotized systems.

MISSION

We are a company of automation and robotics. We are compromised to contribute to this sector, its development growth to a national and international level; employment generation; training in vanguard technology; and contribute the economic value of the society.

VISION

To be a leading company. To have the best resources and infrastructure. To be economically consolidated. We aspire to be able to offer our services such as engineering of the instrumentation, automation, and the control of process equipment, which will allow our clients achieve their production standards in a proper and reliable way.
